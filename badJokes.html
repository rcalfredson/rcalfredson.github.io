Some bad jokes

I'm a very stationery person; my favorite way to travel is by mail.

An ornithologist was studying the life cycles of fleas that live in the feathers of crows. She did not have any fancy equipment, but since the birds were smart, she trained them to count the number of fleas on each other and tap out the result with their beaks in Morse code. A friend asked, "Wouldn't it be easier on the crows if there was some technology to help out with this?" Picking up one of the crows, the scientist replied, "Eh, I've got the next best thing: my crows cope."
